Empowering Women Through Technology

Our comprehensive suite of safety features designed for women, by women.

Emergency SOS

Send instant alerts to emergency contacts with your exact location with just one tap. Works even with limited connectivity and sends continuous location updates.

Trusted Circles

Create groups of friends and family who can monitor your journey in real-time and receive automated alerts when you arrive safely or if you need assistance.

Voice Activated Alerts

Trigger emergency protocols hands-free using custom voice commands when you're unable to access your phone. Works even when your phone is locked.

Safe Route Planning

Get recommendations for safer routes based on community feedback, lighting conditions, and historical safety data to avoid potentially dangerous areas.

Safety Camera

Discretely record video and audio with automatic cloud backup in emergency situations. Evidence is securely stored and accessible to trusted contacts.

Gesture Controls

Set up custom gestures that can activate emergency protocols without needing to unlock your phone. Simple, discrete, and effective in high-stress situations.
